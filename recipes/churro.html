Churros

This is a recipe for churros!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • vegetable oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup white sugar combined with 1 teaspoon cinnamon (for coating)

Preparation:

  1. In a saucepan, combine water, sugar, salt, and 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  2. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the flour until the mixture forms a smooth ball of dough.
  3. In a separate pan, heat vegetable oil for frying.
  4. Transfer the dough to a piping bag fitted with a star tip.
  5. Pipe strips of dough directly into the hot oil, using scissors or a knife to cut the dough. Fry until churros are golden brown.
  6. Remove the fried churros from the oil and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. While the churros are still warm, roll them in a mixture of white sugar and cinnamon, ensuring an even coating.
Churros are best enjoyed warm. Serve them on their own or with a side of chocolate or caramel sauce for dipping. Enjoy! :)
