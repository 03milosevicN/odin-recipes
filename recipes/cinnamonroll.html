Cinnamon rolls

This is a recipe for delicious cinnamon rolls!

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour

Cinnamon filling:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Cream cheese frosting:

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Preparation:

Dough:

  1. In a bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add sugar, melted butter, salt, and eggs to the yeast mixture. Mix well.
  3. Gradually add flour, one cup at a time, stirring until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place until doubled in size (about 1 hour).

    6. Cinnamon filling:

  6. In a small bowl, mix together softened butter, brown sugar, and ground cinnamon to create the filling.

    7. Assembly:

  7. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface into a rectangle (about 16x12 inches).
  8. Spread the cinnamon filling evenly over the dough.
  9. Starting from one of the long edges, tightly roll the dough into a log.
  10. Cut the log into 12 equal slices.

    11. Baking:

  11. Place the slices in a greased baking dish, leaving a little space between each.
  12. Cover the dish with a cloth and let the rolls rise again for about 30 minutes.
  13. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  14. Bake the cinnamon rolls for 20-25 minutes or until they are golden brown.

    15. Frosting:

  15. While the rolls are baking, prepare the frosting by mixing softened butter, cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.

    16. Finishing:

  16. Once the cinnamon rolls are out of the oven, let them cool for a few minutes.
  17. Spread the cream cheese frosting over the warm rolls.
Enjoy! :)
