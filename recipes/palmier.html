Palmiers

This is a recipe for Palmiers cookies!

Ingredients:

Dough:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • Recommended: 2 tablespoons of ground cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to the temperature specified on the puff pastry package.
  2. Sprinkle a clean surface with a layer of granulated sugar.
  3. Roll out the puff pastry sheet on the sugared surface
  4. Sprinkle a generous layer of sugar over the puff pastry. Use a rolling pin to press the sugar into the pastry.
  5. Fold both sides on the puff pastry towards the center, creating a double fold. Then fold it again, bringing one side over the other.
  6. Using a sharp knife, slice the folded pastry into 1/2 inch thick pieces.
  7. Place the slices on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, leaving some space between each piece.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until the palmiers cookies are golden prown and have caramelized edges.
  9. Allow the palmiers to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
    10. Serve with either a cup of coffee or tea. Enjoy! :)
